## Ingredients

- Garlic and Ginger paste
- A few branches of Thyme
- A medium sized Onion, chopped finely
- 2 teaspoons of Oil
- 2 tablespoons of chopped Tomatoes
- 200 grams of Lentils (black or brown)
- 1 Carrot, cut finely or grated
- Salt to taste
- Water

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the lentils**: Wash your lentils with cold water.

2. **Pressure cook**: Transfer your lentils into a pressure cooker. Add some oil and let it cook for around 20 minutes.

3. **Make the base**: In a separate pan, add ginger and garlic paste, and onions, and let cook for 2 minutes.

4. **Add aromatics**: Then add your tomatoes and thyme for 1 or 2 minutes, before transferring the mixture into the lentils.

5. **Simmer**: Let the mixture cook for 5-10 minutes, then serve while hot.
