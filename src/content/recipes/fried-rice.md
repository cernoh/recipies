## Ingredients

- 3 cups of Basmati Rice
- Water
- 500 grams of Chicken Breast
- 4 Eggs
- 1 big carrot (or 2 small ones)
- 150 grams of Cabbage
- Oil
- 2 or 3 sprigs of Spring Onions
- Pepper
- MSG
- Oyster Sauce
- Light and Dark Soya Sauce
- Prawns (optional) → Fish sauce (optional)
- 2 or 3 cloves of Garlic

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the rice**: Cook your rice beforehand, and let it cool for at least an hour. Old rice is better.

2. **Cook the chicken**: Heat your wok, and once it's reached a hot enough temperature, add some oil. Add your chicken, and along with it add garlic, oyster sauce, light and dark soya sauce, and black pepper. The latter 3 should be added to taste.

3. **Fry the eggs**: Fry your eggs, and put them aside for later.

4. **Cook the vegetables**: Fry your carrots and cabbages together for 2 minutes, then also put these aside.

5. **Fry the rice**: Add 1 tsp of oil to the wok, then put your rice to fry alongside with your MSG. Add Light and dark soya sauce to taste, along with a pinch of salt, and heapings of oyster sauce. Add fish sauce if you're including prawns. Cook this for 3 to 4 minutes.

6. **Combine**: Add your vegetables and chicken to the mix, along with your spring onions.

7. **Serve**: Cut up your fried eggs, and sprinkle them on top before serving.
